Client-Side Processing

Every tool available on ToolThump operates entirely within your web browser on your local machine. When you upload an image, process a video, or transform text, the computation happens on your computer's processor. Your files and data are never sent to, processed by, or stored on any external server. This architecture is fundamental to our commitment to privacy; what happens on your computer stays on your computer.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, log, or store any personally identifiable information (PII). This includes your name, email address, physical address, and IP address. There are no user accounts to create and no databases to store user information. We have no interest in tracking you. Your activity on this website is your own and is not monitored by us.

Anonymous Analytics

To improve ToolThump, we need to understand which tools are being used and how visitors find the site. We use a privacy-focused analytics service that does not use cookies and anonymizes all visitor data. All we see are aggregated statistics, such as "200 people used the Image Converter today." This helps us make better decisions about which features to build and improve without ever knowing who you are.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites that we do not operate, such as our open-source repository on GitHub. Please be aware that we have no control over the content, security, or privacy practices of these third-party sites. We strongly advise you to review the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to add new features. Any modifications will be posted on this page. While our core principle of client-side processing will not change, we encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.
