üíªüîêConsider Using a Password Manager

Remembering dozens of complex and unique passwords can be a daunting task. Password managers are tools designed to help you create, store, and manage your passwords securely. They can generate highly random passwords and fill them in for you automatically.

We recommend considering a reputable password manager such as Bitwarden, which is a popular free and open-source option. Other well-regarded password managers include 1Password, LastPass, and KeePass (for self-hosting). Choose one that fits your needs and security preferences.
