💡How to Use

Follow these simple steps to effectively crop and resize your images. All processing is done securely in your browser.

  • 1️⃣
    Upload Your Image: Click the "Choose Image" button or drag and drop your image onto the preview area. Your image will appear in the main canvas.
  • 2️⃣
    Adjust Crop & Resize Options: Choose between "Free Crop" to manually select any area, or "Fixed Ratio" for predefined aspect ratios (e.g., 1:1, 16:9). You can also input target dimensions.
  • 3️⃣
    Process & Download: Once satisfied with your selection, click "Process & Download Image". The tool will crop and resize your image, then automatically download it to your device.
