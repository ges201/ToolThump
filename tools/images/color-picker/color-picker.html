💡How to Use

Follow these simple steps to grab any color from your image. All processing is done securely in your browser.

  • 1️⃣
    Select Your Image: Click the "Upload Image" button to choose a file from your device. Your image will appear in the main canvas area.
  • 2️⃣
    Activate the Eyedropper: Press the "Activate Eyedropper" button. This enables color selection mode and brings up the live magnifier.
  • 3️⃣
    Pick Your Color: Move your mouse over the image to see the magnifier and color preview update in real-time. Use the zoom controls or arrow keys for fine-tuning. Click to select the final color and instantly see its HEX, RGB, and HSL codes.

  • 🔒
    Completely Private: All color processing happens in your browser. Images are never uploaded to a server, ensuring 100% privacy and security.

    Zoom & Pan Controls: Easily navigate high-resolution images. Zoom in for incredible detail and pan across the canvas to find the perfect area.
  • 🎯
    Magnifying Glass: A live, magnified preview of the area under your cursor allows you to select the exact pixel you need with confidence.
  • ⌨️
    Keyboard Navigation: After activating the eyedropper, use your keyboard's arrow keys to nudge the selection one pixel at a time for ultimate precision.
  • 🎨
