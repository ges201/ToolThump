💡How to Use

Follow these simple steps to convert your images. All processing is done securely in your browser for maximum speed and privacy.

  • 1️⃣
    Select Your Images: Use the "Choose File" button for a single image or "Choose Folder" for batch processing. The tool intelligently finds all supported images (JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP) within a folder and its subdirectories.
  • 2️⃣
    Choose the Format: From the dropdown menu, select your desired output format: PNG, JPEG, WEBP, or GIF.
  • 3️⃣
    Convert and Download: Hit the "Convert & Download" button. Single files will download immediately. Multiple files from a folder will be conveniently packaged and downloaded as a single ZIP archive.

✨Key Features

Our Image Converter is designed for speed, privacy, and power. Here's what makes it stand out:

  • 🔒
    Completely Private: All conversions happen in your browser. Images are never uploaded to a server, ensuring 100% privacy.
  • 📂
    Bulk Image Converter: Process an entire folder of images at once, saving time and effort.
  • 🔄
    Recursive Folder Processing: The converter finds images in all subfolders. You can keep the original folder structure in the final ZIP file.
  • ⚡
    Fast & Efficient: Using your computer's power, conversions are incredibly fast. Get images ready in seconds.
  • 🗂️
    Folder Structure: When converting a folder, you can check the "Retain folder structure" box. If enabled, the original hierarchy of your subfolders will be recreated inside the final ZIP file.
  • ⚪
    Handling Transparency: When converting an image with a transparent background (like a PNG) to a format that doesn't support it (like JPEG), the transparent area will be automatically filled with a solid white color.
